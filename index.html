Born and bred in Argentina. I lived in Barcelona for 5 years and moved to Berlin in December 2020. Throughout my life I have worked in different fields. Since I like many things, I am always learning something new and I am a very self-taught person, always up for new challenges. Nowadays I am concentrating on web development but never leaving aside my audiovisual passion. The first time I got in contact with web development was back in 2018 when I created my first website with wordpress from scratch. When I moved to Berlin I was determined to become a web developer, and signed up at Le Wagon. I graduated in June 2021 and I haven't stopped coding and practicing ever since. My goal is to keep learning and improving to get involved in cool, creative and challenging projects.
